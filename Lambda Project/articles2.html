Porsche 911 GT3

14th July 2024

The Porsche 911 GT3 is a high-performance sports car renowned for its track-oriented capabilities. Designed with lightweight materials and advanced aerodynamics

It includes rear-axle steering and Porsche's Active Suspension Management for exceptional handling.

It features a naturally aspirated 4.0-liter flat-six engine producing around 502 horsepower, with options for a 7-speed PDK dual-clutch or a 6-speed manual transmission.

The GT3 combines its motorsport heritage with modern engineering to deliver an exhilarating driving experience on both road and track.
